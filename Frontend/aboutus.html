About CUET Blood Bank

Connecting donors with recipients to save lives across Chittagong

Our Story

Founded in 2022, CUET Blood Bank emerged from a simple observation: the critical need for an efficient blood donation system in our community.

What started as a student project has grown into a trusted platform connecting hundreds of donors with those in urgent need of blood transfusions.

Today, we're proud to serve the entire Chittagong region with a network of dedicated donors, medical professionals, and community partners.

Our Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles that drive everything we do

Mission

To create a reliable, efficient and compassionate blood donation ecosystem that ensures no patient dies due to lack of blood in Chittagong.

Vision

A Bangladesh where safe blood is always available for every patient in need through voluntary, non-remunerated blood donation.

Goal

To register 10,000 voluntary blood donors by 2025 and reduce blood shortage in Chittagong by 50%.
