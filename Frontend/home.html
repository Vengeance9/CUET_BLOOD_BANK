DO YOU KNOW

Facts

The process is relatively painless, with only a slight prick felt during needle insertion

A healthy body replenishes the lost blood volume within 48 hours, with no long-term effects

All equipment used is sterile and single-use, ensuring no risk of infection

Blood banks need a constant supply for emergencies and chronic conditions like cancer or anemia

Vegetarians have sufficient nutrients, including iron, to maintain healthy hemoglobin levels

Myths

Donating blood is painful

Donating blood weakens the donor

You can catch diseases like HIV from donating

Blood donation is only needed during emergencies

Vegetarians cannot donate blood
