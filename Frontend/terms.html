Terms & Conditions

Welcome to CUET Blood Bank Management System! By using our platform, you agree to the following terms:

  1. This system is designed to connect blood donors and recipients in times of emergency.
  2. All users are expected to provide accurate and honest information (such as blood type, contact details, availability).
  3. Misuse of this platform (e.g., sharing false data or spamming) may result in removal from the system.
  4. We do not charge any fee for blood donation or requests. It is a voluntary service.
  5. This is a student project and not an officially registered healthcare service.

Additional Information

The CUET Blood Bank Management System is developed by students of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET) as part of an academic project. While we strive to provide accurate and reliable services, we cannot guarantee the availability of donors or the accuracy of the information provided by users.

We encourage all users to verify information before proceeding with any blood donation or request. The platform is intended for emergency situations and should not replace professional medical services.

CUET Blood Bank

CUET Blood Bank is an innovative platform designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients, ensuring timely access to life-saving blood donations during emergencies.
